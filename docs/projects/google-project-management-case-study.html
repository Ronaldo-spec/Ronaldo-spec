Case context

Business objective

Sauce & Spoon is a restaurant chain testing menu tablets to improve service speed, collect operational data, and support future rollout decisions.

01

Align scope and goals

Documented project summary, goals, deliverables, scope, benefits, cost considerations, and stakeholder misalignment through a project charter.

02

Evaluate stakeholders

Assessed stakeholder power, interest, communication needs, and coalition opportunities to support scope control and decision-making.

03

Plan work and milestones

Identified tasks such as vendor selection, Wi-Fi testing, staff training, troubleshooting plan, change management, testing, and SOP preparation.

04

Measure quality and close the project

Defined quality standards, evaluation questions, customer survey logic, retrospective findings, senior stakeholder communication, and closeout reporting.
