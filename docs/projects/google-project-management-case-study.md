# Google Project Management Case Study: Sauce & Spoon Menu Tablet Rollout

This portfolio artifact summarizes a Google Project Management capstone-style case study based on the **Sauce & Spoon menu tablet rollout** scenario.

The original assignment document covers project charter preparation, stakeholder analysis, coalition communication, project planning, task and milestone identification, quality standards, evaluation planning, retrospective documentation, senior stakeholder communication, and closeout reporting.

---

## Case Study Context

Sauce & Spoon is a small restaurant chain that wants to support growth and expansion by testing menu tablets at selected restaurant locations. The pilot aims to help restaurants serve more guests in less time, collect useful operational data, improve the customer experience, and support business decision-making.

---

## Business Objective

The project objective is to plan and evaluate a menu tablet pilot rollout that can improve restaurant operations and support expansion decisions.

Key business goals include:

- Reduce table turnover time.
- Reduce guest wait time.
- Improve order accuracy.
- Reduce negative customer reviews.
- Support staff training for the new tablet workflow.
- Provide useful data for future rollout decisions.

## Key Planning Elements

### Project Charter

The charter work focused on documenting:

- Project summary
- Goals
- Deliverables
- Scope and exclusions
- Benefits and costs
- Misalignment among stakeholders
- Attachments and supporting references

### Project Tasks

Examples of identified project tasks:

- Coordinate branding and customization of the tablet menu interface.
- Gather stakeholder needs and evaluate project requirements.
- Research and select a suitable tablet vendor.
- Create a statement of work and vendor agreement.
- Install and test Wi-Fi connection readiness.
- Create training material for tablet menu usage.
- Prepare troubleshooting and technical support plans.
- Conduct tablet system testing.
- Create a change management plan for staff.
- Prepare standard operating procedures for cleaning and maintaining tablets.

### Quality Standards

The project included measurable service-quality expectations:

| Quality Area | Target |
|---|---|
| Average ticket time - appetizer | 8 minutes |
| Average ticket time - entree | 12-15 minutes |
| Payment time | 1 minute or less |
| Order accuracy | 98% |
| Weekly technical issue reports | Less than 5% of customers |
| Lobby wait time | Less than 10 minutes |
